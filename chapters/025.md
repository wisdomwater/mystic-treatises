## 25 — Let Love Become Your Rule

> *The truest measure of devotion is a heart made gentle by love.*

Do not measure your spiritual life by extraordinary practices or by the admiration of others. The most beautiful soul is not the one that appears remarkable, but the one whose love has quietly transformed every ordinary action. Prayer that does not soften the heart has not yet reached its purpose. Fasting that does not awaken compassion has not yet borne its fruit. Whatever you do for God, let it make you more patient, more generous, and more ready to forget yourself for the sake of another.

If God grants you zeal, guard it with humility. Do not imitate the outward practices of those whose roots have grown deep before God, while neglecting the quiet virtues that sustain them. Every soul has its proper measure and season. What strengthens one person may burden another. Therefore, seek not to appear holy, but to become faithful. Let your growth be hidden, as a seed grows silently beneath the earth before anyone sees its fruit.

Do not allow your love of prayer to become an excuse for hardness toward others. Welcome those whom God sends to you with kindness, but do not surrender your heart to idle distraction. There is a time to speak and a time to be silent, a time to receive a guest and a time to return to the secret place of prayer. Wisdom is not found in choosing one against the other, but in letting love govern both. The heart that truly seeks God will learn how to protect its stillness without losing its gentleness.

If you must correct another, do so with humility. If you must refuse something, do so with peace. If you are interrupted, let your first response be charity. Better to lose your comfort than your love. Better to shorten your solitude than to wound a brother's heart. For the One you seek in silence is the same Lord who comes to you in every person made in His image.

Remember this above all: the deepest prayer is not merely spoken with the lips or performed by the body. It is prayed with the whole person. When your heart is moved, your body joins it naturally. When love fills the soul, every act becomes worship. Seek that hidden life, and all your outward works will become radiant with the quiet beauty of Christ.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Fill my heart with love.*

> **Exhale:** *Let all I do honor You.*
