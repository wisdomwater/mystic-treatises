## 98 — Love Must Become Visible

> *The love hidden in the heart is made complete when it becomes mercy in the hands.*

Love begins in the secret place of the heart, where God teaches the soul to see every person through His own compassion. Yet love is not meant to remain hidden forever. When the opportunity is given, it longs to become visible. A prayer offered in secret and a hand extended in mercy belong to the same love. Do not separate what God has joined together. The heart that truly loves Him will rejoice whenever it can bring comfort to another.

There are seasons when God calls a soul into deep solitude, where its service is almost entirely hidden. If He has truly placed you there, do not be troubled because your love cannot always be expressed through outward deeds. He sees the compassion you carry within, and nothing offered to Him in sincerity is forgotten. But if He places a suffering person within your reach, do not pass by while claiming devotion to prayer. Love must become flesh whenever the moment calls for it.

Be careful not to use spiritual freedom as a covering for comfort or indifference. It is easy to mistake self-protection for holiness. Humility asks a different question: "How can I serve Christ in the person before me?" When you stoop to care for the weary, the lonely, or the sick, your soul is quietly guarded from pride. Mercy keeps the heart soft. Compassion preserves the soul from the illusion that it has already arrived.

Do not seek a life that is admired. Seek a heart that resembles Christ. Every calling has its proper measure, and God asks only that you be faithful in the place where He has set you. If today He calls you into silence, enter it with your whole heart. If today He calls you to interrupt your silence for the sake of love, go with the same willingness. The two are not enemies. They are the two hands of the same life in God. Walk in humility, and you will discover that every act of love, whether hidden in prayer or revealed in service, leads you more deeply into the heart of Christ.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Make my heart like Yours.*

> **Exhale:** *Teach me to love in deed.*
