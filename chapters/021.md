## 21 — Keep Seeking

> *The one who continues seeking God has already begun to find Him.*

Do not become weary because you have not yet reached the fullness of what you long for. The soul grows slowly into the light. At first, your heart is divided. Love for God rises within you, yet old habits still call you back. This is not the end of your journey but its middle path. Do not despise it. Continue to nourish your heart with the Scriptures, with prayer, with silence, and with every thought that turns you toward God. If you stop seeking, your mind will quickly drift toward lesser things. But if you persevere, even from a distance you will begin to feel the warmth of the coming dawn.

Do not fear if the mysteries of God remain hidden from you. They are not opened by cleverness but by humility. Your task is not to force the door but to wait faithfully before it. Read the holy words. Meditate upon them. Pray with longing. Guard your heart from distractions. The One who holds the key knows the right moment to welcome you further in. What He delays, He does not deny. He prepares the soul before revealing deeper things.

If your earthly life should end before you have seen all that you hoped to understand, do not think your seeking has been wasted. Many of the righteous walked by faith without seeing the fulfillment of every promise. Yet they were not disappointed. They loved what they had only glimpsed from afar, and that hope shaped their whole lives. It is better to spend your days walking toward the light than to settle contentedly in the shadows because the light seems distant.

Keep your heart turned toward God. Let your thoughts dwell often upon Him. Let holy longing become stronger than idle curiosity, and prayer more constant than distraction. Every step taken in humility draws you nearer, even when you cannot perceive the distance closing. The God you seek is already seeking you, quietly drawing your heart by His grace.

Walk on, then, without anxiety. Seek Him with patience, trust Him with the hidden things, and leave the unveiling to His perfect love. No sincere longing for God is ever lost. Every desire offered to Him becomes another step toward the home your soul has always been seeking.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Draw me nearer, Lord.*

> **Exhale:** *I will keep seeking You.*
