## 4 — The Clear Fountain

> *When the heart is no longer filled from outside, its own hidden waters begin to rise.*

Do not think of the world only as the things around you. The world also lives as a current within the heart: the love of possessions, the hunger for praise, the fear of the body, the desire to rule, the envy that follows honor, the restless need to be seen. Where these currents still move in you, there the world still has life. Where they have grown quiet, the world has begun to lose its power.

Learn, then, to discern where you are still bound. Do not do this with harshness, but with truth. Look gently at the movements of your mind. Notice what it seeks without effort, what it returns to again and again, what stirs it toward anxiety, anger, vanity, or fear. These movements reveal the places where you are still living according to the world, and also the places where grace has already made you free.

When the waters from outside no longer pour into the fountain of the soul, its own hidden waters begin to rise. Then thoughts of God come quietly, without force. A light appears that you did not manufacture. You discover wisdom within you that no one placed there by argument. You cannot explain how it came, or who taught you, or why the heart suddenly knows what the tongue cannot fully say.

This is because the soul was not made for darkness. God did not create His image as a captive of passions. The soul’s true nature is clear, spacious, and able to receive blessed light. What darkens it is not its deepest truth, but what has entered from outside and settled there. When the soul returns to its proper order, it rises again toward God.

So do not despair over what still troubles you. The affections that disturb you are not your truest self. Beneath them is a fountain God has not abandoned. Be still. Guard the heart. Let the outer waters grow quiet. In time, the living water within you will rise again, and by its light you will remember where you belong.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Clear the fountain of my heart.*

> **Exhale:** *Let Your living water rise.*
