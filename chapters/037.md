## 37 — Give Your Heart Eyes

> *The heart must be guarded not by fear alone, but by a holy delight stronger than distraction.*

Do not underestimate the power of sacred song. When the mind is restless and scattered, spiritual words can gather it again. They turn the heart away from the noise of the world and give it something living to hold. If you cannot yet remain in silent contemplation, then let psalms, hymns, and holy remembrance become a gentle wall around your thoughts.

Learn also to watch the hidden order of your soul. Spiritual joy is often born after the pain of the cross, while the sweetness of sin usually rises from comfort without watchfulness. The mind is swift and easily carried away. If it is not bound to God by discernment, it will wander from one image to another and lose its strength. Therefore, give your attention something worthy: Scripture, prayer, remembrance of death, hope of the kingdom, and the quiet duties of your place.

Pray that your way of life may be given eyes. Without insight, even good practice can become heavy and dull. But when God gives the heart understanding, labor becomes sweet, troubles become like honey, and the narrow place of obedience becomes a wedding chamber. Then you begin to see that every small struggle has meaning, and every hidden act of faithfulness is preparing the soul for love.

Do not ask for high spiritual delights while neglecting the path that makes the heart able to receive them. God has chosen that we should eat this bread with sweat, not from harshness, but from mercy. Gifts received too easily can become sickness in the soul. But gifts received through humility, patience, solitude, and prayer become healing.

So guard your heart with tenderness and firmness. Let holy words keep you when silence feels too difficult. Let small disciplines protect you when great temptations seem far away. The Lord is not withholding Himself from you. He is preparing you to receive Him with joy. Remain faithful, and the world will loosen its hold as divine love quietly takes its place.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Give my heart eyes.*

> **Exhale:** *Draw me into Your love.*
