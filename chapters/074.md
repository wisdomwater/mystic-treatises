## 74 — The Hidden Gift Within Every Trial

> *The trials that humble you are often the very gifts that strengthen you.*

Do not think that temptation comes only to wound you. In the hands of God, even the things that trouble you can become instruments of healing. Gold is not purified without fire, and the heart is not made steadfast without being tested. The Lord allows His friends to pass through seasons where they discover both their weakness and His faithfulness. In this way they learn not merely to believe in His help, but to know it by experience.

When God allows your strength to fail, He is not abandoning you. He is teaching you where your true strength has always been. Left to yourself, you discover how fragile you are. But when you cry out to Him and He upholds you, your confidence no longer rests in your own resolve. It rests in the mercy of God. This is a knowledge that no book can give and no easy life can produce.

Do not despise the seasons that expose your poverty. They lead you into humility, and humility draws you close to the heart of God. There you learn to pray with perseverance rather than presumption, to hope rather than demand, and to wait without despair. Every temptation patiently endured becomes a teacher. It reveals your need, uncovers hidden pride, strengthens faith, and trains your soul to recognize the quiet help that God has been giving all along.

The Lord deals with each soul according to its need. Some are tested so their virtues may deepen. Some are awakened from spiritual sleep. Some are drawn back when they have wandered. Others, already near to God, are purified until they can stand before Him with greater freedom and confidence. His purposes are never cruel. The medicine may be bitter, but it is given by the Physician who desires your life.

So do not lose heart when the cup before you tastes bitter. Even your endurance is a gift from God. You are but a clay vessel, yet His fire is making you strong enough to carry living water. Ask Him humbly. Wait upon Him faithfully. The One who permits the trial is the same One who will sustain you through it, and in the end you will discover that His grace was present in every step of the journey.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Strengthen me through this trial.*

> **Exhale:** *Your grace is enough.*
