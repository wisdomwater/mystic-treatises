## 11 — The Door of Stillness

> *When you make room for God, He fills the silence with His own life.*

Do not imagine that prayer is merely another duty to add to your day. There are moments when God Himself opens the door of your heart and gently draws you inward. When those moments come, do not rush away because of lesser concerns. Let your work wait if it can. Let your mind rest from its endless planning. Kneel before the Lord without hurry, and remain there as long as love invites you.

Do not measure prayer only by the words you speak. There are times when, as you pour yourself out before God, something deeper awakens within you. Joy rises where there was only longing. Peace flows through your heart like living water. Your strength grows quiet, your thoughts fall silent, and your whole being is gathered into the presence of God. No book can fully describe this, just as no written page can place the taste of honey upon your tongue. These things must be received, not merely understood.

If you desire this life, seek it faithfully. Knock without growing weary. Return to prayer again and again, not to earn God's favor, but because your soul was created for communion with Him. As earthly anxieties loosen their grip and unnecessary burdens fall away, the sweetness of the Spirit finds room to dwell within you. Little by little, the love of this passing world gives way to the joy of the world that is already drawing near.

Do not lose heart if you have not yet tasted these depths. God does not withhold His grace until you have become perfect. The smallest turning of the heart toward Him is precious in His sight. Whenever you reject what darkens your soul and choose what is good, you will discover His mercy meeting you there. He forgives more quickly than you imagine, strengthens more gently than you expect, and leads you, step by step, into the life for which Christ has already come to find you.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Open my heart, Lord.*

> **Exhale:** *Fill me with Your presence.*
