## 6 — Ask for the Kingdom

> *The heart that desires great things learns to pray beyond small fears.*

Learn to pray with wisdom. Do not bring God only the narrow anxieties of the body, as though the Father did not already know your needs. He cares for the birds who neither store nor bargain. How much more will He care for you? Lift your prayer, then, toward what is worthy of His generosity. Ask for the Kingdom. Ask for righteousness. Ask for a heart made spacious by love.

Your prayer reveals your desire. What you seek from God shows what you believe is precious. If your heart is bound to passing things, your petitions will remain small. But when the soul begins to thirst for Jesus, it asks for what cannot decay. It asks for peace that can reign within. It asks for freedom even while still clothed in the body.

Do not be troubled if God delays what you request. You are not wiser than God. Sometimes your life is not yet ready to carry what your mouth has asked. Sometimes your heart is moving in one direction while your prayer is speaking in another. Sometimes the gift is too great to be received quickly, for what is gained without labor is easily despised.

Do not reject the troubles that lead you toward knowledge. Do not fear the trials through which hidden treasures are found. Pray not to be overcome by temptations of the soul, but prepare yourself with courage for the sufferings that purify the heart. Beyond them lies divine rest.

So thirst after Jesus, and He will satisfy you with His love. Close your eyes to the glitter of the world, and spiritual joy will begin to rise. Ask greatly, live humbly, endure patiently, and the Father who knows your need will add what is necessary in mercy.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *I seek Your Kingdom.*

> **Exhale:** *Satisfy me with Your love.*
