## 32 — What God Never Created

> *What love did not create, love will not preserve forever.*

Do not imagine that sin, death, or hell belong to the eternal nature of God. They did not begin in Him, nor do they reveal His heart. Sin is born wherever the human will turns away from the light. Death entered as a bitter medicine for a wounded creation, not as the Creator's final desire. Even all that now grieves you is held only for a time within the wisdom of God.

Do not be afraid, then, as though darkness were equal to light or evil could endure forever beside the goodness of God. Whatever opposes divine love has no life of its own. It exists only because the human heart has wandered from its true home. Just as a shadow disappears when the sun rises, so everything born from separation will vanish when creation is fully restored to the One from whom it came.

When you see the brokenness of the world or the disorder within your own heart, do not despair. Turn your will again toward God. Every movement of love is already a return to life. Every act of repentance is a step out of darkness and into the light. The Lord does not delight in your fear. He delights in your healing. He patiently calls you back, not because His love has weakened, but because it has never ceased.

Remain a humble disciple all your days. Do not imagine that you have outgrown the wisdom of those who walked before you. Receive their words as a child receives bread from loving hands, not to admire the bread itself, but to be nourished by it. As your heart is fed by truth, seek not merely to enjoy holy words but to discover the living God from whom those words flow. The sweetness of prayer, the beauty of Scripture, and the wisdom of the saints are all invitations to know Him more deeply.

So place your confidence in the goodness of God. His purpose is life, not death; healing, not ruin; mercy, not abandonment. Trust the One whose love existed before your first breath and whose faithfulness will remain when every shadow has passed away. Rest in that hope, for the end of God's work is not destruction, but restoration.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Your love is eternal.*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me into Your life.*
