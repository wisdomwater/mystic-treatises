## 54 — The Harbor of Love

> *Fear teaches the soul to return, but love is the place where it comes to rest.*

Faith opens the door to mysteries that cannot be seen with ordinary sight. As the eyes of the body behold the visible world, so the eyes of faith behold the hidden kindness of God. Little by little you begin to recognize His wisdom woven through creation, His quiet care surrounding your life, and His presence drawing you beyond what your natural understanding can grasp.

Do not despise repentance, for it is not merely sorrow over sin. It is a new birth. In repentance the heart returns to the mercy from which it wandered, and God receives it again with tenderness. Fear first awakens the soul and teaches it to turn away from death. It is like a faithful guide leading a traveler through dangerous country. But fear was never meant to be your home. It leads you until it has brought you to something greater.

That greater thing is love. Adam lost this treasure and learned to labor among thorns. We know the same labor whenever we try to live apart from the love of God. Even our good works become heavy when they are carried by our own strength. But when divine love is found, the soul begins to feed upon Christ Himself. What was once weariness becomes nourishment. What was once striving becomes communion. The heart discovers a life that no earthly bread can sustain.

Think of repentance as a ship. Let holy fear be its rudder. It carries you across the restless sea of this world, through storms and uncertainty, until at last you arrive in the harbor of divine love. There the voyage ends. There the heart finds the One it has been seeking all along.

Remain faithful to the journey. Do not turn back when the sea is rough. The God who first awakened your heart will also bring you safely to Himself. And when love has become your dwelling place, you will discover that every step of the journey—even repentance itself—was an expression of His mercy calling you home.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Lead me through mercy*

> **Exhale:** *Into Your perfect love*
