## 47 — Every Victory Is Grace

> *The soul remains safe when it remembers that every good gift comes from God.*

If you have struggled long against temptation, do not let your victories become a cause for pride. The enemy delights as much in your success as in your failure if either one turns your eyes away from God. Remember how easily a dry leaf falls from its branch. So too your own strength would fail in a moment if the Lord withdrew His sustaining hand.

Consider how often others long for the same holiness you desire, yet continue to struggle under burdens they cannot overcome. Do not judge them, nor imagine yourself stronger. If you have been preserved, it is because mercy has surrounded you. If you have endured, it is because God has quietly upheld you. Every temptation resisted and every good work completed bears the fingerprint of His grace before it bears your own.

Learn, then, to thank God more than you congratulate yourself. Gratitude protects the heart where pride leaves it exposed. The more clearly you see that every virtue has been nurtured by the Lord's kindness, the more deeply you will love Him. Humility does not diminish your labor; it reveals the One who has been laboring beside you all along.

Do not fear your weakness. Fear only the illusion of self-sufficiency. Your weakness, entrusted to God, becomes the place where His strength is most clearly seen. Walk forward, then, with confidence—not in yourself, but in the faithful love of the One who has carried you to this day and who will not abandon the work He has begun within you.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *You are my strength.*

> **Exhale:** *Keep me humble.*
