## 73 — The Quiet Way of Trust

> *God's greatest wonders are often hidden within ordinary faithfulness.*

Do not seek extraordinary signs from God. Do not ask Him to prove His nearness by wonders or visible power. He already cares for you more faithfully than you can imagine. Most of His works are hidden, like roots beneath the earth or living water flowing beneath the desert sand. His providence is quiet because His love is secure. He does not neglect you simply because He acts in secret.

When your strength begins to fail, do what you can with the strength you have been given. Pray. Labor. Wait. Entrust yourself to God without demanding that He remove every burden. If your need truly exceeds your strength, He knows it long before you ask. At the proper moment He will provide what is needed—not always in the way you expected, but always in the way that leads you nearer to Him.

Learn from the saints. They did not presume upon God's power, though they knew He could do all things. They did not seek miracles to spare themselves effort. They walked until they could walk no farther. They prayed until they had no words left. Then they placed themselves into the hands of God and rested there. Sometimes He answered in ways beyond imagination. More often He strengthened them quietly to take one more step. In either case, they were content because they sought God Himself rather than His gifts.

Do not mistake comfort for spiritual maturity. The Spirit teaches the soul through watchfulness, perseverance, and willing love. There is a holy weariness that comes from serving God with all your heart, and within that weariness there is a hidden joy that cannot be found in an easy life. The Lord prepares His children not by removing every struggle but by forming in them steadfastness, wisdom, and compassion. The rest your heart longs for is real, but it belongs to the kingdom that is coming. For now, let love make you faithful in today's small obedience.

Walk quietly. Trust deeply. Do not envy those whose lives appear easier than your own. Every unseen act of faith, every hidden prayer, every humble labor is precious before God. He sees what no one else sees, and His gentle care never leaves those who place themselves entirely into His hands.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *I trust Your hidden care.*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me one step more.*
