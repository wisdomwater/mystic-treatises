## 31 — Carry Your Pearl with Hope

> *Guard the small beginnings of grace, for they lead to treasures beyond imagining.*

Do not be surprised by the changing seasons of the soul. There will be days when your heart overflows with repentance, and the thought of death awakens you to what truly matters. There will also be days when waves rise suddenly against you, and your little vessel seems ready to sink beneath storms you never expected. Do not think that either season means God has abandoned you. Those who have sailed the deep waters of solitude know that both calm and tempest belong to the journey.

Guard the treasure that has been entrusted to you. Do not allow the distractions of this world, the praise of others, or the love of passing comforts to steal the pearl that God is forming within your heart. Great joys are preserved through many small acts of faithfulness. No child begins with solid food. In the same way, no soul is entrusted with greater mysteries before learning to cherish the simple work of daily obedience, prayer, and humility.

Do not grow weary because your progress seems slow. Every step toward God reveals a beauty greater than the one before it. What fills you with wonder today will one day seem only the beginning. The Lord leads His children from light to greater light, from one measure of love to another. Therefore, ask only that the next door may be opened. Leave tomorrow's gifts in His hands.

Above all, resist discouragement. You do not serve a harsh master who is eager to condemn you. You serve the Lord who loved you before you existed, who called you into being, and who delights to give good gifts to those who seek Him. If He delays, it is not because He has forgotten you, but because hope itself is becoming part of your healing. Hope strengthens weary hands. Hope gives patience to prayer. Hope makes even difficult paths lighter because it teaches the heart to expect the goodness of God.

So remain where you have been placed today. Pray with expectation. Work with quiet perseverance. Carry your pearl carefully through this passing world. The One who planted longing within your heart is already preparing the joy that will satisfy it forever. His mercy will not fail you before you reach the harbor.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Strengthen my hope.*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me safely home.*
