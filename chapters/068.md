## 68 — Keep the Holy Fire Alive

> *The heart remains watchful when love for God burns brighter than every other love.*

When God awakens love within your heart, He also gives you a holy zeal to guard it. This zeal is not harshness, nor is it anger toward others. It is the quiet strength that refuses to let your love grow cold. Like a faithful watchman, it stands at the door of the soul, keeping watch over the treasure that has been entrusted to you. Without this inner vigilance, even the sincerest desire for God can slowly fade.

There is a fear that comes from unbelief, and there is a fear that comes from love. Do not confuse the two. The first is anxious for itself. It doubts God's care, imagines danger everywhere, and exhausts the soul with restless worry. But the second is a gift from God. It is the gentle concern of a heart that longs not to lose its closeness to Him. It watches over prayer. It guards humility. It notices the small compromises that quietly draw the soul away from love. Such fear does not imprison the heart—it protects it.

This holy watchfulness grows stronger as your love grows deeper. Yet it can fall asleep when you begin to presume upon yourself. The soul says, "I am safe now," and gradually lays aside its vigilance. It becomes occupied with countless earthly concerns. It seeks the approval of others. It fills its days with distractions until the bright flame that once burned within begins to grow dim. Rarely does the heart drift from God all at once. It is more often lulled to sleep by many small forgetfulnesses.

Therefore, return often to the simplicity of His presence. Guard your heart more carefully than your reputation. Nourish your love more diligently than your achievements. If you find the flame burning low, do not despair. Turn again toward the One who first kindled it. He has not ceased to watch over you. As you draw near to Him, He will breathe upon the fading embers until they burn once more with quiet, steadfast fire. His desire is not merely that you begin the journey with zeal, but that your love may endure to the end.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Kindle my heart, Lord.

> **Exhale:** Keep my love awake.
