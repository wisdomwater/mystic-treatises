## 2 — The Heart That Can Hear

> *Only a heart made spacious by mercy can recognize the voice of God.*

Do not be discouraged if words about God seem distant or lifeless to you. Spiritual truth is not grasped by cleverness, but by the condition of the heart. A mind crowded with endless concerns cannot easily receive what is quiet and eternal. If your thoughts are always occupied with passing things, the beauty of God's life will seem faint. But whenever you make room within yourself, even a single word carrying the fragrance of the Spirit can awaken a deep longing that has been waiting there all along.

Do not wait for extraordinary grace before taking the next step. Sometimes God grants a soul unexpected gifts, but more often He leads us gently along the ordinary path. Loosen your grip on what cannot last. Turn your attention again toward Him. Walk faithfully in the small acts of love set before you. Each quiet act of obedience becomes another step up the hidden tower whose summit is communion with God.

If you desire to know God beyond ideas, let mercy become your constant companion. Mercy shapes the heart into the likeness of its Creator. Every act of compassion, every refusal to condemn, every kindness offered without seeking reward polishes the soul until it begins to reflect the divine light. The beauty of holiness is not formed by harsh discipline alone, but by a heart that learns to love as God loves.

Do not trust words that have never become life. It is easy to speak beautifully about prayer, humility, or love while remaining a stranger to them. Seek instead the wisdom that has been tested by tears, silence, repentance, and faithful practice. Such words carry living water because they rise from a soul that has drunk deeply of God. Let your own life become that kind of testimony, where your actions speak before your lips do.

So return again to mercy. There, more surely than anywhere else, you will find the doorway into God's own heart. And as you learn to dwell in mercy, you will discover that He has already been dwelling within you.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Make my heart spacious.*

> **Exhale:** *Teach me Your mercy.*
