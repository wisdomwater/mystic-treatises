## 58 — The First Awakening

> *Grace often begins by teaching the heart that nothing passing can finally satisfy it.*

The first touch of God's grace upon the soul is often quiet and easily overlooked. It comes as a gentle awareness that everything in this world is passing away. What once seemed permanent begins to appear fragile. What once captivated the heart no longer satisfies it. Do not be afraid of this awakening. It is not the loss of life but the beginning of it.

Guard this holy thought carefully. Do not smother it beneath endless distractions, constant activity, or the pursuit of things that cannot endure. The enemy fears this awakening because he knows that when the heart truly understands the passing nature of earthly things, it begins to seek the One who alone remains forever. If this seed is cherished in silence and prayer, it will grow into a wisdom beyond words.

As this grace deepens, another gift is given. You begin to perceive, not merely that the world is passing away, but that the love of God is holding all things together. His care reaches every creature. His mercy surrounds every life. Then the heart is set aflame with a love that cannot be explained. At times it becomes so overwhelmed by God's goodness that every other thought falls silent. The soul rests in Him as though it had finally come home.

Do not imagine that such moments are earned. They are gifts. Your part is simply to remain faithful: to pray, to watch, to keep your heart free from needless distractions, and to welcome the grace God entrusts to you. Protect the small beginnings, for great trees grow from hidden seeds.

If, then, the Lord places within your heart a longing for what is eternal, receive it with gratitude. Do not hurry past it. Sit quietly with Him. What begins as a simple awareness that this world cannot satisfy will become, by His mercy, a joyful vision of the love that never passes away.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Awaken my heart*

> **Exhale:** *To Your eternal love*
