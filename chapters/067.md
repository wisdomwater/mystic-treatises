## 67 — There Is a Guardian With You

> *Courage grows when the heart remembers it is never alone.*

When fear comes to you, do not answer it with more fearful thoughts. Instead, remind your heart of this: there is a Guardian with you. Nothing can touch your life apart from the loving wisdom of God. Neither the malice of people, nor the schemes of the evil one, nor the uncertainties of tomorrow have authority over you apart from His providence. Rest, then, beneath His care. What He permits, He also accompanies with His grace.

Do not imagine that God delights in your suffering. He does not ask you to seek pain for its own sake. What He desires is your love. The trials that meet you on the path are not proof that He has abandoned you, but opportunities to entrust yourself more deeply into His hands. Every burden carried in love becomes a place where His hidden treasures are revealed. Those who remain faithful through hardship discover a peace that cannot be found in comfort alone.

Do not become discouraged if your struggles seem long. Many who have walked this road before you endured years of dryness before they tasted the sweetness of God's consolation. Yet they did not lose heart. They continued in prayer, trusting what they could not yet feel. In time, the soul that perseveres discovers that rest was quietly growing beneath the surface all along. Grace often ripens slowly, like fruit hidden beneath the leaves, until the day when its sweetness is suddenly revealed.

Guard your silence as carefully as your words. A quiet tongue strengthens the heart for prayer, and a heart turned steadily toward God begins to see the world differently. There are moments when His presence becomes so gentle and so full that time itself seems to disappear. Do not seek such experiences for their own sake, but receive them with gratitude if they are given. Seek only God. Whether He leads you through labor or through consolation, remain near Him with confidence. The One who watches over you neither sleeps nor grows weary, and His faithful care will never leave you.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** You are my Guardian.

> **Exhale:** I rest in Your care.
