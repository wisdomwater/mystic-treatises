## 94 — Keep Company with the Saints

> *The heart grows strong when it remembers those who have walked the way of God before it.*

If weariness comes while you are praying, let your body rest for a little while, but do not let your heart become idle. Lift your thoughts toward the goodness of God. Remember the mercy that called you away from empty pursuits and invited you into the life of prayer. Consider how small your labor is compared with the joy God has promised those who seek Him. Let hope become the companion of your waiting. No hour spent with God is ever lost, even when it feels hidden from your own eyes.

Then let your mind travel among the saints. Walk with those who loved God more than comfort. See them leaving behind the noise of the world to seek the silence where the heart can hear His voice. They found caves, mountains, deserts, and lonely cells more beautiful than palaces because they discovered there the presence of the One they loved. They gave up much, yet they were never abandoned. The God who called them also sustained them, providing what they needed in ways both ordinary and wondrous. He proved Himself faithful again and again.

Do not dwell only on the hardships they endured. See also the grace that carried them. They faced loneliness, temptation, weakness, and long years of hidden faithfulness, yet divine love upheld them through it all. When food was scarce, God provided. When strength failed, He renewed them. When darkness pressed upon them, His presence became their light. The same Lord who cared for them has not changed. He knows your needs just as surely as He knew theirs, and His mercy has not grown weary.

As you remember these holy lives, allow your own heart to awaken. Courage rises where despair once lingered. Weariness gives way to quiet joy. Tears become gentle companions instead of burdens. Hope steadies the soul, and the mind grows clear again. You may not dwell in a desert cave, yet you can carry the same longing within your heart. Walk faithfully where God has placed you, keeping company with those who have gone before you in spirit. Their witness reminds you that every step toward God is met by His grace. As you remain with Him, even the darkest night begins to shine with the light of His presence, and your soul discovers that heaven has already begun within the heart that loves Him.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Strengthen my heart, O Lord.*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me in the way of Your saints.*
