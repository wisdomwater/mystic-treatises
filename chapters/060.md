## 60 — Choose the Better Way

> *Whenever two paths lie before you, choose the one that looks most like Christ.*

Do not seek to win. Seek to love. It is better to suffer wrong than to do wrong. Better to be misunderstood than to wound another. Better to bear the burden than to place it upon someone else's shoulders. If you cannot lift your brother, at least do not become the one who causes him to stumble.

Cover the faults of others as you would wish your own to be covered. If you cannot carry another's weakness, then at least do not expose it. Speak gently before you give generously. Correct in secret rather than in public. Weep with those who weep, rejoice with those who rejoice, and let your heart become a place where every weary soul may find kindness instead of judgment.

Do not wait until you have become perfect before you begin walking toward God. If you cannot quiet every thought, then guard your tongue. If you cannot yet be alone in heart, be faithful in outward stillness. If you cannot fast greatly, avoid excess. If you cannot be victorious today, then refuse at least to surrender. Every small step toward the light weakens the hold of darkness.

Above all, pursue peace. Do not become a lover of arguments or a guardian of your own importance. Humility gives birth to peace, peace gives birth to joy, and joy teaches the heart to rest in God. The soul that has found hope no longer needs to prove itself, for it has entrusted itself to the mercy of the Lord.

Remember that Christ carried the cross before you. He welcomed sinners before they repented perfectly, forgave before they deserved it, and answered hatred with love. Walk after Him. Let hope become stronger than fear, mercy stronger than judgment, and love stronger than every injury. Then, even while you remain in this world, your heart will begin to dwell in the peace of His kingdom.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Lead me in Your peace*

> **Exhale:** *Teach me to love like Christ*
