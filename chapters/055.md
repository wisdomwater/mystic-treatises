## 55 — From Hearing to Seeing

> *Faith begins by listening to God, but love opens the eyes of the heart.*

God has placed within you a quiet witness that knows the difference between what leads to life and what leads away from it. Before anyone teaches you, your heart already bears this gentle light. Do not silence it. It is one of God's first gifts, drawing you toward Him before you even know His name.

When you follow that inward light, faith begins to awaken. Faith gives birth to holy reverence, and reverence leads the soul into repentance. Do not think of repentance as the end of the journey. It is the doorway through which you begin to receive what God has long desired to give. As the heart is cleansed, hidden things slowly become clear. What was once only believed begins to be quietly perceived.

There comes a time when faith is no longer only hearing. It becomes seeing. Not with the eyes of the body, but with the eyes of the heart. You begin to recognize the wisdom of God where you once saw only confusion. You perceive His mercy beneath His providence. You discover His presence hidden within ordinary life. This is not knowledge you achieve. It is a gift given to the humble soul that continues to walk with Him.

Guard, then, the small beginnings. Cherish the gentle convictions that lead you toward goodness. Welcome repentance when it calls you home. Do not grow weary if your progress seems slow, for every faithful step prepares the heart for a greater vision. Love waits beyond fear, and peace waits beyond striving.

Walk faithfully with the light you have today. The God who first taught your conscience to recognize the good will, in His time, open your heart to behold His beauty. Then what you once knew only by faith will become the quiet joy of living in His presence.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Open the eyes of my heart*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me into Your light*
