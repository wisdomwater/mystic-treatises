## 3 — The Kingdom Within

> *The mercy you practice becomes the light by which you begin to see.*

Do not imagine the Kingdom of God as only a faraway promise. The delight prepared for the age to come has already left its pledge within you. What is now partial will one day be complete. What you now glimpse as through a dim glass will one day shine before you without shadow. Even now, the Spirit gives the heart a taste of that future joy, not through the senses of the body, but through the quiet awakening of the soul.

If you wish to become a friend of excellence, do not measure yourself only by the good things you eagerly choose. Learn also to receive patiently the hard things that come to you. It is a great thing to practice beauty; it is greater still not to be shaken when difficulty clings to your path. Freedom is proven not only when you are able to act, but when your heart remains steady before what would disturb it.

Protect the sinner without crushing him. Strengthen his courage for life, and the mercy of the Lord will carry you. Speak a sustaining word to the weak and the sorrowful whenever you can, and the hand that upholds the universe will uphold you. Share in the grief of those who suffer. Pray with them from the heart. Before you even finish asking, a fountain of grace will begin to open.

Be watchful in prayer. Keep your gaze turned toward Scripture with understanding, so that idle thoughts do not scatter your attention. Do not test yourself by entertaining what you know can wound you. Even the wise have stumbled where they thought themselves secure. Show your weakness to God at all times, so that you are never left alone with your own strength.

The way of God begins quietly within. When prayer grows heavy, return gently. When zeal fades, begin again. When darkness approaches, do not despair. Humility, faith, and the confession of the heart are small lamps, but they are enough for the next step. And as you walk by their light, mercy will lead you deeper into the Kingdom already hidden within you.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Your Kingdom is within me.*

> **Exhale:** *Let mercy become my light.*
