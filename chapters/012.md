## 12 — Entrust Yourself to God

> *The heart that rests in God's care is freed from the burden of carrying itself.*

Do not trust too quickly in your own strength. Until your faith has been tested, you do not yet know how weak or how strong you are. Let every trial teach you humility rather than confidence in yourself. Do not rely upon your knowledge, your discipline, or your past victories, for these can vanish in a moment. Instead, learn to lean upon God, whose faithfulness never changes. A humble heart stands more securely than the strongest will.

Know this also: God's care surrounds you at every moment, though you may not always perceive it. Those whose hearts have been purified by continual remembrance of God begin to recognize His quiet presence in every circumstance. Sometimes His help comes unseen. Sometimes He strengthens the soul with a peace that cannot be explained. Whether you notice His hand or not, He does not abandon those who entrust themselves to Him. His love is nearer than your fears.

If you truly believe that God cares for you, why should anxiety become your master? Cast your cares upon Him, and your heart will discover a peace that no earthly security can provide. The soul cannot enter stillness while it clings tightly to every possession, every plan, and every outcome. As your hands gradually loosen their grip, your heart becomes free to receive what God has long desired to give. Faith grows not through certainty about tomorrow, but through repeated trust in the One who holds tomorrow already.

Do not turn away from the one who asks for your help. If you have received something, receive it as a gift entrusted to you for the comfort of another. Rejoice when you are able to give, for God has allowed you to become a small reflection of His generosity. And if you have nothing to offer but your own poverty, rejoice even then. The poor who trust God are never forgotten. Whether you possess much or little, you remain rich in the love of the One who delights to care for His children. Place yourself into His hands, and you will find that He has been carrying you all along.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *I place my life in You.*

> **Exhale:** *You are my faithful Keeper.*
