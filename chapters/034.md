## 34 — Taught by the Light

> *What is planted by God within you still needs the light of God to grow.*

Do not imagine that evil belongs to your nature. Goodness was planted in you from the beginning. The heart may become darkened, confused, and wounded, but darkness is not its first truth. What is foreign must be taught from outside; what is from God unfolds from within. Even when the good in you appears dim, it has not been destroyed.

Yet the good that is planted in you must still be awakened, strengthened, and illumined. A seed contains life, but it needs sun and rain. So your mind naturally leans toward the good, but it does not come to fullness without grace. God sends light through many servants: through Scripture, through the saints, through the holy angels, through every word and movement that turns the soul toward life. What comes from light gives light. What comes from darkness leaves the heart more divided.

Learn, then, to discern the teachers that approach your soul. Every influence invites you to become like itself. The holy powers lead you toward humility, purity, mercy, and truth. Dark powers may be swift and clever, but swiftness is not illumination. A mind may move quickly and still move toward ruin. True light does not merely excite the heart; it reveals things as they are and makes the soul more whole.

Do not be troubled that you receive divine things through many intermediaries in this world. We are children learning the language of heaven. One soul helps another. Higher lights pour themselves into lower lights. Grace descends gently, according to what each can bear. But in the world to come, this schooling will give way to direct delight. There, no one will need to borrow another's lamp, for the one Giver will reveal Himself to all.

Even judgment itself will be known in the light of love. Those who have resisted love will feel the pain of having sinned against the One who loved them, and that grief will be sharper than fear. Those who have welcomed love will be made drunk with its joy. So turn toward the light now. Let your life bear witness that sin has lost its sweetness. Trust the mercy that teaches, purifies, and heals, until every heart is drawn into the love from which it was first made.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Awaken Your light within me.*

> **Exhale:** *Teach me the way of love.*
