## 22 — Trust the Hidden Seasons

> *The God who seems hidden is often doing His deepest work.*

Do not be alarmed when the light you once enjoyed seems to fade. There are seasons when your heart feels clouded, your prayers seem dry, and the joy that once came so freely is nowhere to be found. Do not conclude that God has left you. Just as the sun is hidden behind passing clouds without ceasing to shine, so the grace of God remains near, even when you cannot perceive it. Stay where you are. Continue in prayer. Continue in the Scriptures. Continue to wait with quiet patience.

In these hidden seasons, do not force yourself into despair, nor demand that consolation return at once. Simply place yourself before God again and again. The words of prayer, however faint they may feel, become like a gentle wind that scatters the clouds from your heart. Little by little, the darkness gives way. Then, often when you least expect it, joy returns—not because you have mastered the way, but because God has visited you with His mercy.

When this consolation comes, receive it with gratitude but not with pride. Tears may spring from your eyes without effort. Your heart may burn with love, and prayer may become sweeter than any earthly pleasure. These are not rewards for your achievement. They are gifts from the Spirit, given to strengthen you on the journey. Welcome them humbly, and when they pass, continue faithfully. Seek the Giver more than the gifts.

Know that the life of the soul unfolds gradually. Faithful labor gives rise to holy longing. Holy longing awakens deeper prayer. Prayer softens the heart until tears begin to flow. Tears prepare the way for peace. And from peace comes a quiet clarity by which the soul begins to perceive the hidden goodness of God. You cannot hurry this work any more than you can command a seed to become a tree overnight. Your task is only to remain faithful.

So do not lose heart when the sky grows dark, and do not cling too tightly when it grows bright. In every season, God is leading you. Whether through clouds or through sunlight, His purpose is the same: to draw your heart into a peace that no passing shadow can ever take away.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Lead me through every season.*

> **Exhale:** *I will wait for You.*
