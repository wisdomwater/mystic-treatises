## 69 — Walk a Little Farther

> *The soul is transformed, not by standing still, but by taking one faithful step after another.*

The heart cannot belong to two worlds at once. As you give yourself more deeply to the things that pass away, they quietly claim your attention, your affection, and your strength. But as your mind turns toward the kingdom that is to come, another love begins to grow within you. The beauty of what is eternal gradually overshadows the charm of what is temporary. You need not force yourself to despise the world; simply learn to love God more.

Do not expect this change to happen all at once. Every journey is made one step at a time. A traveler who never leaves his place will never reach his destination, no matter how much he longs for it. So it is with the soul. If you wish to live more fully in God, you must continually move toward Him. Even the smallest faithful step weakens the hold that lesser things have upon your heart.

Do not burden yourself with anxious care for tomorrow. Provide for what is necessary with gratitude and simplicity, but refuse to let your needs become your master. Trust your Father more than your own calculations. He knows what is required for your life before you ask Him. When you loosen your grip on earthly concerns, you do not become careless—you become free. The hands that cling tightly to this world cannot be fully opened to receive the kingdom.

Pray, then, that the love of God may become the strongest affection within you. Let it bind your heart with a bond that nothing in this passing world can loosen. As that love grows, many other attachments will quietly fall away. You will discover that you have not lost what truly matters, but found it. For the soul that belongs to God is already tasting, even now, the life that will one day be known in its fullness. Walk a little farther today, and let His love draw you onward.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Draw me closer, Lord.

> **Exhale:** My heart belongs to You.
