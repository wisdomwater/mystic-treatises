## 28 — Beyond Visions

> *Do not seek extraordinary experiences. Seek the God who is beyond every image.*

Do not imagine that every vision, dream, or powerful spiritual experience is the same as knowing God. The Lord has often spoken through dreams, signs, and images because they are suited to our weakness. They awaken us, guide us, and strengthen our faith. Receive such gifts with gratitude if they are given, but do not mistake the gift for the Giver. A sign points beyond itself. It is not the destination.

There is a deeper way by which God makes Himself known. It does not depend upon outward images or extraordinary experiences, but upon a quiet illumination within the heart. In that stillness, the mind is gently opened to truths that no picture could contain. Such knowledge cannot be seized or manufactured. It is received in humility, as grace slowly enlarges the soul's capacity to bear the light.

Even those who have received remarkable revelations have often known God only in part. Do not be troubled by this. The mystery of God is greater than every human understanding, and His wisdom cannot be exhausted by the brightest mind or the holiest saint. What He reveals is always true, yet it is only enough for today. He gives each soul what it can faithfully carry, inviting us to continue walking by trust rather than by certainty.

Therefore, seek purity of heart more than unusual experiences. Seek love more than visions. Seek humility more than hidden knowledge. As your heart is made simple and your life is quietly offered to God, He will reveal Himself in the measure that is good for you. And even when much remains hidden, you have lost nothing. To know that His mercy is beyond your understanding is already to stand at the threshold of true wisdom. Rest there with peace, for the God who cannot be fully comprehended can always be fully trusted.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Open my heart to You.*

> **Exhale:** *I rest in Your mystery.*
