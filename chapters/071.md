## 71 — The Strength That Humility Gives

> *Only the humble heart can safely carry the gifts of God.*

If God entrusts you with wisdom, joy in prayer, or any other grace, receive it with gratitude, but hold it gently. Every gift is safest in the hands of one who knows that it is still a gift. Ask God to teach you humility as faithfully as He teaches you holiness. Riches of the soul, like riches of the world, can be lost by those who believe they possess them by their own strength. The deeper your roots grow into humility, the more securely every grace will remain.

Do not be surprised when the path toward God includes hardship. Affliction is not the enemy of the spiritual life but its faithful companion. Patience gives birth to humility, and humility keeps the heart turned toward God in constant prayer. If your days become difficult, do not conclude that God has abandoned you. Rather, remain near Him. Many hidden treasures are revealed only to the soul that has learned to bow low beneath the weight of trial.

Guard your heart more carefully than your reputation. Pride rarely arrives shouting its name. It enters quietly, through the pleasure of being admired, through confidence in your own understanding, through the secret desire to appear wiser, holier, or stronger than you are. Do not be afraid because these thoughts visit you. They become dangerous only when you welcome them and make them your companions. Let them pass by like clouds. Return again to humility, and the enemy will find no place to rest.

If ever you find yourself surrounded by darkness, when prayer seems empty and certainty slips through your fingers, do not trust your own strength. In such moments even the wisest mind discovers its weakness. Cling instead to humility. It is stronger than cleverness, stronger than spiritual experiences, stronger than every argument you can make within yourself. The humble heart is hidden within the mercy of Christ, where no darkness can finally overcome it.

So ask for nothing more earnestly than a humble spirit. Let love of God remain greater than love of your own progress. Whether your path is filled with light or shadow, with consolation or temptation, remain small before the Lord. His mercy is a shelter that never fails, and those who rest beneath it will never walk alone.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Keep me humble, Lord.*

> **Exhale:** *Hide me in Your mercy.*
