## 61 — The Rest That Hope Gives

> *Peace does not come from escaping life's burdens, but from placing your hope in God.*

If you desire peace, become a person of peace. Do not seek wealth when you should be asking for wisdom. Do not long for honor when humility is the garment that clothes the soul in beauty. Seek neither power nor greatness. Seek only the quiet heart that has learned to rest in God.

Know this: humility gives birth to true understanding, and understanding leads the heart into peace. Where there is no humility, wisdom remains distant. Where there is no peace, humility has not yet taken root. Many people spend their lives wandering from one pursuit to another, believing that the next accomplishment, possession, or pleasure will finally quiet their restless hearts. Yet peace is never found along those roads. It is found only when the soul turns toward hope in God.

When hope awakens within you, your heart begins to grow still. Fear loosens its grip. Joy quietly returns, not because every burden has disappeared, but because you no longer carry those burdens alone. Did not our Lord invite the weary and the heavy laden to come to Him for rest? Leave behind the countless paths that promise satisfaction but cannot give it. Draw near to Him who is Himself your rest, and you will discover a peace the world cannot create.

Faith is born when the light of God begins to dawn within the heart. From faith comes hope, and hope gives strength to endure every trial. There are seasons when this light seems hidden and fear rises again. Do not lose courage when this happens. Even these moments have their purpose, for they teach humility and call you back to repentance. The One who carried the cross has opened the way for every sinner to return without fear.

Never doubt the mercy of God. A heart that turns toward Him with sincere humility will never be turned away. Your sorrow for your sins is not the end of your story but the doorway through which grace enters. Rest there today. Let hope lift your heart once more, and trust that His mercy is greater than all your weakness.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Lord, You are my rest.

> **Exhale:** My hope is in You.
