## 17 — Cross the Sea with Hope

> *The one who keeps his eyes on God reaches the harbor without counting every wave.*

Do not be afraid to begin the work that God has placed before you. If you wait until every danger has disappeared, you will never take the first step. There will always be storms to imagine, reasons to delay, and questions without answers. But the soul that entrusts itself to God does not measure every wave before setting sail. It fixes its eyes upon the One who calls, and hope becomes stronger than fear.

Many become weary before they have even begun. They spend their strength calculating every possible hardship, searching for certainty that this life cannot give. But faith is not born from mastering the future. It is born from placing your future into God's hands. The traveler who constantly stops to count the dangers remains standing at the shore. The one who walks in humble trust discovers that grace has already gone before him.

When you set your hand to any good work, do so with your whole heart. Do not keep one foot turned toward retreat. Offer yourself as one who belongs entirely to God, remembering that this passing life is brief and that the life to come is everlasting. Then your labor will no longer feel burdensome, for you will not be working merely for earthly success, but out of love for the One who first loved you. God measures your offering less by its greatness than by the faith with which you give it.

There are many paths by which God leads His children. One is drawn into tears. Another into silence. Another into fervent prayer. Another into patient endurance. Do not compare your journey with another's, nor become proud if you taste some sweetness along the way. Every gift is grace, and every grace is given to draw you nearer to Christ. Receive what He gives with gratitude, persevere when the way seems hidden, and leave the outcome to Him.

Walk on with joy. Begin today. Trust more than you calculate. The God who called you is already waiting at the harbor, and He will faithfully bring to completion the work He has begun within you.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *My hope is in You.*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me safely home.*
