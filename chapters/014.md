## 14 — Choose the Lower Place

> *Whoever delights in becoming small will discover the greatness of God.*

Do not be troubled when others overlook you, misunderstand you, or fail to honor you. If you can bear these things without bitterness, your heart will become quiet, and in that quietness you will begin to see the truth. The one who gladly accepts being thought little by the world is no longer imprisoned by the world's opinions. Such freedom is precious in the sight of God, for humility opens the soul to a joy that pride can never know.

Do not chase after honor, for it will always flee from those who pursue it. Instead, choose the lower place. Be the first to greet others. Think of yourself less, and think well of those you meet. If God desires to lift you up, no one can prevent it. But if you seek to exalt yourself, you will carry a burden that was never meant to be yours. Leave your reputation in God's hands, and let your heart rest there.

Learn to see every person with mercy. Love sinners, but do not love the things that wound them. Do not despise anyone because of their weakness, for you wear the same human nature and stand in need of the same mercy. When another stumbles, do not rush to condemn. Speak gently, as a physician who longs to heal rather than to shame. Love does not delight in exposing another's faults. It longs only for their restoration.

Practice honoring others, even when they seem unworthy of honor. Speak words that awaken goodness in them. Let your kindness become an invitation for them to become the person God already sees. You will find that as you sow peace into another's heart, peace quietly takes root within your own. In learning to bless instead of accuse, to encourage instead of diminish, your soul itself is transformed.

For where humility begins to grow, the glory of God quietly appears. The humble heart is never empty. It is filled with the gentle presence of the One who became a servant for our sake. Walk that same path without fear, and you will discover that the lowest place in the kingdom is already filled with the light of heaven.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Clothe me with humility.*

> **Exhale:** *Teach me to love as You love.*
