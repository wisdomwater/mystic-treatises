## 65 — When Faith Becomes Light

> *Faith reaches its fullness when it becomes an inner light rather than merely an outward belief.*

Do not think that faith is complete simply because your mind agrees with what is true. Such faith is a good beginning, but God desires something deeper for you. There is a faith that is born within the heart itself—a quiet light given by grace. It does not arise from argument or from hearing the testimony of others. It dawns gently within the soul until hope becomes more certain than fear, and trust grows stronger than every doubt.

This gift cannot be produced by effort alone. You may prepare your heart through prayer, humility, and faithful obedience, but the light itself is always God's gift. When it comes, it reveals what could never be discovered by reasoning. The soul begins to perceive the hidden nearness of God. It learns that it has never walked alone. The Comforter quietly opens the eyes of the heart, and what was once believed from afar becomes known through living experience.

As this light grows, love begins to overcome fear. The soul no longer clings so tightly to its own safety, for it has learned to trust the One who holds all things. It becomes willing to surrender every earthly security because it has found a greater treasure. Lifted by faith, the heart rises above the endless noise of this passing world and tastes, even now, something of the peace and joy that belong to the kingdom of heaven.

Yet remember that what you experience here is only the first glimpse. We do not yet behold God face to face. We walk by faith, not by sight. Even so, these quiet moments of illumination are a precious pledge of what is to come. Receive them with gratitude, and do not seek to grasp them as your own possession. Let them awaken deeper love, greater humility, and a more steadfast hope. The God who has begun to reveal Himself to you will complete His work in His perfect time, and the light that now visits your heart will one day become the everlasting day in which you live forever.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Shine within me, Lord.

> **Exhale:** Lead me into Your light.
