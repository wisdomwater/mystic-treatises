## 88 — The Soul's Hidden Spring

> *When the heart is made pure, grace awakens what God has already planted within it.*

There are many gifts that God may grant in answer to your prayers. He may strengthen you in trials, give you wisdom, comfort your heart, or sustain you in your labor. Yet there are deeper gifts that cannot be seized by effort alone. They belong to the soul that has been quietly purified by love. These mysteries are not rewards for achievement, but the natural flowering of a heart made ready to receive them.

Grace works within the soul as spring works upon the earth. All winter long the hidden roots remain unseen, yet when warmth returns they suddenly awaken, and the fields are clothed with life. So it is with the soul. God has placed within you treasures that have long been concealed beneath fear, distraction, and the passions. As these coverings are gently removed, grace does not create something new; it reveals the beauty that God has hidden there from the beginning.

When the soul begins to behold this beauty, it is drawn toward God with quiet wonder. Earthly things lose their power to captivate because a greater beauty has been discovered within the light of his presence. The soul becomes like a clear mirror, no longer clouded, but reflecting the radiance for which it was created. This is not cause for pride, for the beauty you behold is not your own accomplishment. It is the image of God, patiently uncovered by his mercy.

Do not hurry toward lofty experiences. Seek purity instead. Many virtues may grow in the midst of ordinary life, but the deepest stillness requires a heart that willingly makes room for God. Whether your solitude is found in a quiet room, a silent morning, or the hidden chamber of your heart, guard that sacred place. There grace performs its quiet work, often unseen even by you.

The pure heart does not strive to manufacture heavenly light. It simply remains open. And in God's own time, the light comes of itself. Just as spring does not force the flowers to bloom but gently awakens what was already alive, so the Spirit reveals the life that has been hidden within you. Trust this patient work. The One who planted his image in your soul is faithful to bring its beauty into the light.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Awaken Your life within me.*

> **Exhale:** *Let Your beauty bloom.*
