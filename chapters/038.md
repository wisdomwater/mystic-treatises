## 38 — The Sweet Madness of Love

> *When divine love takes hold of the heart, even suffering becomes a path into joy.*

When the love of God awakens within a soul, it does not always come with explanations. At first it may rise quietly, like a hidden fire in the heart, before the mind understands its cause. It warms the whole person. It loosens fear. It makes the soul bold, tender, and strangely free. The world begins to lose its weight because another love has become heavier than all things.

This love made martyrs joyful in suffering and sent apostles across the earth. It made the wise willing to appear foolish, the cautious willing to become bold, and the comfortable willing to wander in deserts. Do not think this is ordinary emotion. It is a holy wound. The heart that is touched by it can no longer measure life by safety, reputation, or ease. It has seen a Friend more beautiful than all these.

Yet do not trust too quickly in peace before humility has become your dwelling place. If your affections seem quiet, give thanks, but remain watchful. Until the soul enters the city of humility, hidden troubles may still rise unexpectedly. Humility is the true resting place. Every virtue leads toward it, and without it, even high experiences can become dangerous.

A little suffering freely borne for God is more precious than much service performed in comfort. Love is proved not only when the path is sweet, but when the body grows weary and the heart remains faithful. If you desire the glory of Christ, do not refuse the small share of His cross appointed to you. The body learns obedience; the mind learns stillness; the heart learns love.

So ask not merely to feel love, but to know the One who is Love. Pray to be given the gaze that forgets the world because it is captivated by God. Pray to remember the humility of Christ until your own humiliation becomes sweet. And when God lowers you, do not despair. He is not casting you away. He is teaching you the peace that only the humble can carry.

### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** *Kindle Your love in me.*

> **Exhale:** *Teach me humility.*
